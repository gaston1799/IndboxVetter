Privacy Policy

Last updated:

InboxVetter uses Google Sign-In solely for authentication. We receive your basic profile information (name, email, and profile image) from Google to create and maintain your account. We do not request access to your Gmail contents in this demo environment.

Any settings you configure within the app are stored in a local JSON store on the server for demonstration purposes. These include preferences related to vetting automations and UI options. We do not sell or share personal information with third parties.

If you choose to subscribe via Stripe in test mode, payment data is handled by Stripe. We do not store full card details. Please refer to Stripe’s Privacy Policy.

You can request account deletion by contacting support. Authentication sessions last until the Google token expires, after which you may need to sign in again.

For questions, contact: support@inboxvetter.local
